About Greenpath Wellness

Greenpath Wellness was created to provide high-quality, accessible professional development opportunities for social workers in Louisiana and beyond.

We believe in growth through continuous learning. Our workshops and training programs are designed by experienced practitioners who understand the real challenges you face in your work. Whether you're pursuing CE credits, expanding your clinical skills, or preparing for clinical supervision, we offer practical, evidence-based training that makes a difference.

Our current focus is on telesupervision training for Louisiana social workers, but we're expanding our offerings to include additional workshops, recorded courses, and professional development resources.

Meet Your Instructor

Brandon Reeves, LCSW-BACS

Board-Approved Clinical Supervisor

Brandon is a licensed clinical social worker with over 25 years of experience in private practice and clinical supervision. He serves on the Louisiana State Board of Social Work Examiners and maintains a private practice at Baton Rouge Counseling Associates, where he specializes in anxiety, depression, trauma, and life transitions.

As a Board-Approved Clinical Supervisor, Brandon has guided numerous supervisees through the licensure process and understands the unique challenges of clinical supervision in Louisiana.
