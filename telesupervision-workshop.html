What You'll Learn

This workshop provides comprehensive training on telesupervision tailored specifically for Louisiana social workers. You'll gain practical knowledge and tools you can implement immediately in your supervisory practice.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand LABSWE Requirements: Navigate Louisiana State Board regulations for telesupervision, including compliance requirements and documentation standards.
  • Master Virtual Supervision Best Practices: Learn evidence-based strategies for conducting effective supervision sessions remotely while maintaining therapeutic alliance and professional boundaries.
  • Select and Use Technology Tools: Explore HIPAA-compliant platforms, tools for secure communication, and best practices for technology use in clinical supervision.
  • Address Ethical Considerations: Navigate confidentiality, informed consent, emergency protocols, and ethical challenges unique to telesupervision.
  • Apply Real-World Solutions: Benefit from case examples, troubleshooting strategies, and lessons learned from experienced supervisors.

Workshop Format

This interactive 1.5-hour live virtual workshop combines presentation, discussion, and Q&A. You'll have opportunities to ask questions, engage with the presenters, and learn from the experiences of other supervisors.

  • Live presentation via Zoom
  • Slide deck provided for reference
  • Interactive Q&A session
  • CE certificate upon completion
  • Access to supplemental resources

Your Instructors

Brandon Reeves, LCSW-BACS

Brandon is a licensed clinical social worker with over 25 years of experience and serves on the Louisiana State Board of Social Work Examiners. As a Board-Approved Clinical Supervisor, he brings extensive knowledge of LABSWE regulations and practical experience supervising clinicians in private practice settings. Brandon maintains a private practice at Baton Rouge Counseling Associates and provides clinical supervision and consultation throughout Louisiana.

Gina Rossi, LCSW-BACS

Gina is an experienced Board-Approved Clinical Supervisor with a deep understanding of the challenges and opportunities of virtual supervision. She has successfully transitioned her supervisory practice to include telesupervision and is passionate about helping other supervisors navigate this evolution. Gina brings practical insights, ethical considerations, and real-world strategies to help supervisors provide high-quality remote supervision.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to attend live, or is there a recording?

This is a live workshop. Attendance is required to receive CE credit and meet LABSWE requirements for telesupervision training. We do not offer recordings at this time.

Will I receive the slides?

Yes! All registered participants will have access to the slide deck for future reference.

What if I can't make my scheduled session?

Contact us at info@greenpathwellness.com to transfer to a different date, subject to availability.

Does this meet LABSWE telesupervision requirements?

Yes. This workshop is approved by LABSWE and meets the telesupervision workshop requirement for supervisors. It also counts toward your "in-person" continuing education requirement.

What technology do I need?

You'll need a computer, tablet, or smartphone with internet access. The workshop is conducted via Zoom (free account is fine). We recommend using a device with a camera and microphone for full participation.

Is there a group discount?

Yes! Contact Gina or Brandon for group rates if you're registering 5 or more participants from your practice or organization.
